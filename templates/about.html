about us

We Make Custom Insoles

FootBalance is a Nordic, health-driven company. We believe health and well-being begin with your feet, and with our expertise and knowledge, we aim to keep you active, fit, and injury-free. At FootBalance Nepal, we uniquely offer a personalized fit using advanced 3D foot scanning, biomechanical analysis, and product recommendations, featuring our award-winning custom insoles. Our custom insoles help to align your feet, support your arches, and balance your body. Our journey into customized insoles began with significant support from FootBalance (Finland). Besides treating foot problems, we also address posture-related issues and provide physiotherapy treatments.
