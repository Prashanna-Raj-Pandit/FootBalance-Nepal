Every foot is unique

Know your foot

No two feet – not even your own – are the same. Like a fingerprint, every foot is unique, with its own distinct shape and contour. A vast majority, 75% of us, live with a foot imbalance. It affects our everyday lives by creating unnecessary pain and discomfort also in other parts of our bodies and not letting us enjoy the activities and sports we love the most. Here at FootBalance Nepal, we understand this diversity and provide custom insoles crafted specifically to fit your feet perfectly.

Introducing FIT Pro

We are pleased to introduce FIT Pro for the First time in Nepal at Novel Wellness Center. It's a step-by-step guide to the unique FootBalance foot analysis & customised insole moulding process. Using FitPro 3D scanning service allows you to discover precisely what your feet require to improve the overall comfort of your body.
